  • Unripe Dates as Fertility Foods

    • In the open-air market in some parts of Saudi Arabia, you might find young unripe green dates (and the dried version) being sold as fertility foods. I was told by these 'street vendors' that the Bedouin will boil the young unripe green dates or the dried version, and drink the water to help them conceived. It tastes bitter and may not be easy to consume, but if your desire is to conceive, it is worth giving it a try.
